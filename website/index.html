Envision the Future.

Next-generation mobility for low-vision individuals.

A future for everyone.

Autonomous vehicles bring forth a new set of questions in machine-computer interaction, with a lot of exploration currently being conducted on idea interfaces for such vehicles. Autonomous vehicles also open new doors for people with vision impairment, who may find it extremely difficult to travel independently.

Project details

The project will showcase how participants with low vision would interact with an autonomous vehicle. Interaction techniques such as motion-tracking technolgy, voice and touch will be tested to determine the user experience (UX) in this setting.
